Bachelor of Business Administration in Financial Services 

BBA in Financial Services is an undergraduate programme designed to prepare students for a professional qualification in financial services and provides a solid base in the Financial Services domain. The course will also inculcate emerging technology used in financial services to better prepare students to be competent industry professionals.

  • Immersive Internship Programs

  • Strong Industry-Academia Interface

  • Comprehensive & Contemporary curriculum
  • Holistic Career Development Support

  • Practical Training in Business Simulation Lab.

Practical Exposure & Career Development

Real-World Experience:

Internship opportunities with leading financial institutions and collaborations with industry experts for workshops and seminars.

Career Services:

Career counseling and networking opportunities through alumni events and industry partnerships to align academic pursuits with career aspirations. We focus on getting the best brands and high-demand job profiles, giving our students a world of interesting opportunities in a wide range of industries.

Curriculum Overview

Core Objectives:

To provide a solid foundation in business administration with a focus on the BFSI Sector and to develop essential knowledge and skills for sales and operations roles within BFSI.

Curricular Breadth:

General courses in Management, Marketing, Economics, Laws, Entrepreneurship contextualized for BFSI, alongside specialized courses in Banking, Insurance, NBFCs, Mutual Funds, Financial Mathematics, Investment & Securities Markets.

Professional Development:

Courses aimed at Personality Development, English Language proficiency, and Sales Management.

Program Highlights

Duration & Structure:

A comprehensive 3-year journey across 6 semesters.

Internships:

Two strategic internships for real-world experience; a 2-month stint post-Semester 4 and an extensive 4-month engagement during Semester 6.

Certifications:

Esteemed certifications integrated within the curriculum, including Licentiate in Life and General Insurance, Diploma in Banking & Finance, and certifications from NISM.

Placements:

With our connections in the BFSI sector, we can ensure placements for candidates who complete the 3 - year program, these will be largely frontline sales profiles in and around the state of Gujarat.

Admission Criteria & Infrastructure

Selective Admission:

A rigorous process to select candidates with strong academic records and a passion for finance, considering extracurricular activities and leadership experiences.

Robust Infrastructure:

Advanced learning environments and comprehensive resources to support in-depth research and analysis.

Faculty & Resources

Expert Instructors:

A dedicated team of faculty members with a rich blend of academic knowledge and industry experience.

State-of-the-Art Facilities:
